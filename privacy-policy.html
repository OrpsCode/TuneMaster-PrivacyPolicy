Privacy Policy for TuneMaster

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Welcome to TuneMaster ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by OrpsCode. This document explains the limited personal data we handle when you use the TuneMaster mobile application (the "App"). We act as the Data Controller for GDPR and similar laws, and operate with a data-minimalist mindset: we do not profile you or collect personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to run the App's core features, manage subscriptions, and serve advertisements.

1. Introduction

We only process what is required for the App to function: pitch detection, MIDI export, and paid features. The rest of the policy details what we collect, why we collect it, and how we secure it.

2. The Data We Collect and Why

  • Microphone Access: Required to listen to audio and convert it into MIDI notes. Audio is processed locally in real-time; we do not record, store, or transmit your voice or audio data.
  • Subscription Data (via RevenueCat): RevenueCat collects anonymized App User IDs, receipts, and purchase tokens to unlock premium features. We only use this data to verify purchases and gate pro features; we do not store raw payment information.
  • Advertising Data (via Google AdMob): AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or mobile advertising IDs (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA) to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Processors

  • Google AdMob delivers the banner ads shown in the app.
  • RevenueCat manages subscription logic, entitlements, and sandbox testing.
  • These providers host data globally (e.g., United States) under Standard Contractual Clauses or comparable safeguards.

4. Advertising Consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

Users in those regions may see a consent dialog on first launch. Granting consent allows AdMob to use your mobile identifiers for personalized ads. Declining still shows ads but without personalization. You can revoke consent anytime via the App settings or your device controls.

5. Data Retention

  • Audio is processed briefly and immediately discarded; nothing is stored.
  • RevenueCat and the app stores retain subscription records only as long as needed to maintain entitlements and comply with reporting laws.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you can request access to, erasure of, or changes to your data. For subscription details contact us and we will route the request to RevenueCat. Advertising preferences are managed through your device OS (“Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS, “Ads Personalization” on Android) or the consent menu inside the App.

EU/EEA residents may also lodge complaints with their data protection authority (OrpsCode is based in Sweden so you can also contact the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection - Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten). California residents: we do not “sell” or “share” your personal information as defined by the CCPA; data is only shared with processors like Google and RevenueCat for app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

TuneMaster does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or 16 in some EU countries). If we discover such data was collected without consent, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy with future app or legal changes. Any material updates will reflect in the “Last Updated” date above.

9. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights please email us at support.orpscode@pm.me.
